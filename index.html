Imo State, Nigeria

Imo is a state located in the South-East geopolitical zone of Nigeria. It is bordered to the north by Anambra State, to the west and south by Rivers State, and to the east by Abia State. The state is named after the Imo River, which flows along its eastern border. The capital of Imo State is Owerri, and its slogan is - Eastern Heartland.
