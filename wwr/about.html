WhiteWater Rafting

Our Vision

Our goal is to become Africa's premier provider of exceptional white-water rafting experiences, delivering unforgettable adventures that immerse people in nature, support sustainable tourism, and highlight Nigeria's untouched natural beauty.

History

WhiteWater Rafting was established in 2001 by a group of passionate adventurers and water sports enthusiasts united by the goal of bringing the excitement of white-water rafting to Nigeria. Inspired by the adventurous spirit of the world’s top rafting destinations, the founders sought to create unforgettable experiences while showcasing the natural beauty of Nigeria’s rivers. Beginning with a small team and a few basic rafting tools, the company set out to explore the untapped potential of Nigeria’s diverse river systems, including the Niger, Benue, and Imo Rivers.

As demand increased, WhiteWater Rafting expanded its operations, adopting modern safety protocols and high-quality rafting gear to ensure that every guest could enjoy the thrill of rafting in a secure and exhilarating environment. Over the years, the company has built a strong reputation as one of Africa’s premier rafting operators, attracting both local and international adventurers. With a focus on sustainability and community support, WhiteWater Rafting continues to grow, offering exciting expeditions suitable for both beginners and experienced rafters.
